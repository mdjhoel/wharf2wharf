Epic trail run!

Experience beautiful and historic St. Andrews, NB in a whole new way ... from the bottom of the ocean! This trail run begins and ends at the town wharf and circumnavigates the town and Ministers island. Yes, you can actually run to and around an island on this race. The Bay of Fundy has the highest tidal range in the world, permitting an unique outdoor experience.
